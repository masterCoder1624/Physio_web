Dr. Ashutosh Sharma

MPTh/MPT (Neuro), BPTh/BPT

Dr. Ashutosh Sharma is a relentless advocate for non-surgical pain relief. With over 11 years of experience in Neurological and Orthopedic rehabilitation, he currently serves as the Chief Physiotherapist at My Physio Clinic and Associate Professor at JNU Medical College, Jaipur.

He specializes in advanced spine mobilization (Schroth Method), treating complex conditions like scoliosis, slip disc, and paralysis. His evidence-based approach has successfully avoided surgery for over 2000 patients, proving that the right therapy can heal even the most stubborn injuries.
